About us

The "pet protected" project aims to create a situation in which the residents of Israel distribute among themselves all the objects, related to animals, which they do not need, but are still valuable and it is a pity to throw them away. But to this day we have not had access to it. The project is a social business that sustains itself financially, and is also assisted by financial support of the good will of its users. As part of the course, we were given the tools to set up such a venture, as well as make a website accessible to all.
